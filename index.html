Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 30 minutes | Servings: 4

  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 1 cup (110g) self-rising flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 stick (60g) melted butter
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) milk
Time Remaining: 00:00
  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, eggs, melted butter, and milk.
  3. Beat the Mixture: Use an electric mixer to beat the ingredients until the mixture is smooth. This should take about 3 minutes.
  4. Prepare the Cake Pan: Grease a cake pan and line the bottom with baking paper.
  5. Pour the Batter: Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  6. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for about 40 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  7. Cool and Decorate: Allow the cake to cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, you can add frosting, sprinkles, or any other decorations you like.
